# Task: Investigate Sidebar Content Styling

## Objective
Research the sidebar content area - the Room info, Contents list, etc. Understand how it's structured and what's involved in improving spacing/readability. DO NOT MAKE CHANGES - research only.

2. Where are they defined? Where styled?
3. How is the Contents list structured? (ul/li? divs? dynamic?)
4. What controls spacing between items?
5. Are there shared styles or is each tab styled independently?
6. What would "more breathing room" require changing?
7. Any dynamic content that complicates styling?
